Player-Kills (PKs)

  • When a player slays another player without premeditation
  • Requires escalation
  • A player killed this way doesn't receive any canonical repercusions, instead the events happen to an "NPC of similar qualities"
  • Downed players may elect to continue roleplaying before passing into PK, but the rule still applies
  • Players killed this way can be robbed just like a Robbery, but the cooldown still applies
  • There's no cooldown to PKing other players
