Hostility

  • A faction can declare they are hostile to another
  • All the players from the affected factions get a terrible standing with the opposite faction's NPCs
  • This allows a basis of explanation for faction wide protection or attacks against another faction
  • If a faction is hostile against another, the players of these factions MAY NOT wander in the territories of another faction when there's noone of that faction online
  • Hostility opens up the possibility for Faction v Faction events
