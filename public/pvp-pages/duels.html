Duels

  • Players fight each other without the intent to kill IC
  • Still requires escalation
  • The wounds that can affect players at the end of a duel only happen if they are accepted by both parties
  • Neither player can force a PK on the other or on self, it will only happen if it's accepted by both parties
  • Duels are forbidden from escalating into other kinds of PVP
  • Duels have 2 minutes of cooldown
