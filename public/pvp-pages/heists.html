Heists

  • After a Spy of a faction succesfully surveys and then posts out a public roleplay of how they infiltrated a hostile faction's base...
  • The aggressor faction may elect to target a specific coffer (chest) of the defending faction, a screenshot with this chest's contents must be provided by the Spy in question, the defending faction may not change the contents of this chest until the heist is over
  • After at least 30 minutes of preparation, the aggressor faction may penetrate into the defending faction's base, and if they reach that chest, they may take half of any item found within (Up to a maximum of 3 stacks of items)
  • Heists allow for the aggressor faction to use tools to break into the defending base, they may not tunnel beneath, but they can mine through walls to get to the destination
  • Unless the defending faction succeeds at counter-espionage, they may not know they are being robbed, if the defending faction succeeds at counter-espionage they must make it known
  • Heists have a 5 day cooldown
  • Heists cost 100 RP
