Kidnapping

  • To kidnap a player they must be PK'd with blunt weapons first and then helped up
  • To kidnap a player the aggressor needs a holding cell or a prison within their base
  • Only one person from each faction may be kidnapped at a time
  • A player about to be kidnapped can pick one of two choices on how to proceed:
  • KIDNAPPED FOR REAL
  • Their items will be placed inside an admin-chest
  • Ransom will be played out with full roleplay
  • The kidnapepd player must obey what the kidnapper asks them to do
  • Kidnapped players cannot fight back unless the kidnapper allows for it
  • Kidnapped players can switch to the other mode of kidnapping at any time, but not vicecersa
  • A kidnapping may only last up to 2 IRL hours (Unless the kidnapped absolutely wants to continue)
  • NPC STAND-IN
  • An NPC with the player's skin will be placed in the kidnapper's prison
  • Ransom will be played out with full roleplay
  • The "kidnapped" player cannot join any efforts against the kidnapping faction
  • Since NPC pathing is wonky, to rescue an NPC kidnap victim the rescuers must simply defend the location for 2 minutes
  • This still only lasts up to 2 IRL hours
  • Nobody from the affected faction can be kidnapped again for the rest of the day after a kidnapping took place
