Robberies

  • When a player steals items from another player
  • Requires escalation
  • The classic threats to life in exchange for items
  • If the victim agrees to being robbed, they may share a screenshot of their inventory to the robber, the robber can then steal up to 8 items of the rarest item stack, 32 of a medium rarity stacks, and up to a 64 of common goods
  • Robbing someone has a cooldown of 60 minutes, this protection applies to all members of the robbed faction
