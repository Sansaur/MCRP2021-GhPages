Sieges

  • A final attack upon a faction's base
  • The attackers must have won at least ONE field warfare beforehand
  • The defenders cannot leave their base for the duration of a siege
  • The attackers will ask for an amount of items the defender must pay for a siege to end, if the defender doesn't pay, the attack begins
  • During an offensive siege the attacking faction may use explosives to break the OUTER WALLS of a faction's base
  • If the attacker is succesful, they may steal up to five stacks of items of assorted goods from any chest within the faction, they may not steal more than 16 of exceptionally rare materials
  • This is a ticket-based fight
  • Base Tickets are 10 and each side can get additional tickets the following ways:
  • Attackers
  • +5 Tickets for each won Warfare beforehand
  • +5 Tickets if they built encampments on surrounding territories
  • +5 Tickets they succesfully spied the defenders before the siege
  • +5 Tickets they have built a workshop at their base
  • Defenders
  • +10 Tickets for each won Warfare beforehand
  • +5 Tickets if they built a Castle
  • +5 Tickets if they built Barracks
  • +1 Ticket per NPC in their base
  • Sieges costs 100 RP
  • Sieges have 5 days of cooldown AND if the attacker is succesful, hostilty is ended for that same duration with a cease fire
