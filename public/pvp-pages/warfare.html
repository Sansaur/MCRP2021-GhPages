Warfare

  • An offensive onto an enemy faction's territories
  • After a claim on a land has been made, the attackers may arrive there and place spawn points in a nearby area, defenders do too
  • After that a ticket-based fight between the factions begins, if the attacker wins, they take temporary control over the territory and they may build there even during hostility
  • Base Tickets are 10 and each side can get additional tickets the following ways:
  • Attackers
  • +5 Tickets if they ALL have mounts before the start of the battle
  • +5 Tickets if they come from the high ground
  • Defenders
  • +5 Tickets if they have defensive buildings like towers or trenches in the territory
  • +5 Tickets if they had spied the attackers beforehand
  • Warfare costs 100 RP
  • Warfare has 1 day of cooldown
