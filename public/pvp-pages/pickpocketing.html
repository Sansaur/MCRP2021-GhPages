Pickpocketing

  • When a player steals ingame currency from another player
  • DOES NOT require escalation
  • To pickpocket, a player wearing a whitelisted thief headwear, and using the Satchel must stealthily approach another player and hit their backs with the item
  • If the attempt is succesful, the victim must forfeit 30% of their carried ingame currency to the thief, thief may ask for inventory screenshots as proof. After this, the victim and allies cannot attack the thief until it's out of sight
  • If the thief is noticed before the attack by either the victim or one of their allies, the attempt fails and the victim and their allies may freely attack the thief
  • If the thief is noticed AND the victim's faction is hostile to the thief AND the thief is within the victim's territory, the victim may elect to instantly kidnap the thief without the need for a fight
  • Stealing from someone has a cooldown of 30 minutes
