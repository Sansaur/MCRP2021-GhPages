Full Mod list
(Libraries and config mods omitted)

Tools and essentials

  • World Edit (Tools for macro-sized building)
  • Time Control (Days will pass slower)
  • InCharacter (Resurrected by me, maximum range in chat speech and commands for roleplay)
  • Nature's compass (Adds an item that finds biomes)
  • Enchantment description (Some enchantments are explained)
  • Optifine + Better FPS + FoamFix + Random patches (Optimize performance / Add more graphical options)
  • Morpheous (Sleeping isn't cancer)
  • Hwyla (You know what you're looking at)
  • Yung's better caves (Underground caves are almost always interconnected)
  • Forgiving void (Falling out of the map isn't instant death)
  • JEI (See recipes of anything)
  • No recipe conflict (Prevents recipe duplicates from overwriting other recipes)
  • Inventory + Mouse tweaks (Faster ability to control the inventory)
  • AppleSkin (Better control over hunger)
  • JourneyMap (A complete minimap and useful map mod)
  • FTB Utilities (Trash can, friends list, and some admin commands)
  • Carry On (Move chests around without opening them)
  • Player Revive (Instead of dying you're put in a "crit" state, which allows people to RP with you and ress you so they can keep RPing)
  • There are more but they aren't noticeable...

World Building

  • Custom NPCs (Admin-only mod to make NPCs)
  • Torchmaster (Adds giant torches to prevent mobs from spawning in an area, useful for complex bases or bases that are supposed to be dark)
  • Recurrent Complex (More random structures)
  • Quark (A kitchen sink mod of oddities, fun blocks and decorations, apart from new very minor mechanics)
  • Biomes o plenty (Adds +60 new biomes to world generation)

Combat

  • Spartan weaponry + Shields (Adds melee and ranged weapons which have special abilities)
  • Tinker's Construct (IT'S RESTRICTED TO ADMIN-ONLY CONTENT!... Adds the ability to create tools by mixing and matching parts which grant special abilities)
  • Heroic Armory (ADMIN ONLY, meme melee weapons, for museum or collection-purposes mostly)
  • Enchanting Plus (Better enchanting tables that allow for higher levels of enchanting easier)
  • Primitive mobs (Adds hostile mobs that pose a slightly bigger challenge than vanilla mobs)
  • Ice and Fire (Adds hostile mobs that kinda delete players easily)
  • Electroblob's wizardry (Adds the fun kind of magic)
  • Undead rising (Adds hostile mobs that attack players in advantageous circumstances)
  • Matchlock Guns (Adds gunpowder weapons)

Content

  • Chisels and bits (Adds tools which allow their user to create 3D models by mixing tiny-sized voxels made out of other blocks, can also be used for custom armors)
  • Roots (A druidry mod, adds nature-attuned spells and rituals)
  • Bewitchment (A witchery mod, adds satanic rituals, a new way of brewing potions, more potion effects, flying brooms and cursed dolls)
  • Iron Backpacks (Backpacks! A secondary inventory)
  • Totemic (A shamanism mod, adds totems, rituals with song and dance, medicine bags, and animals that give more food)
  • ViesCraft (Airships! BOMBER AIRSHIPS!)
  • MxTunes (Play MML Music in minecraft with instruments)
  • Pam's Harvestcraft (Plant plants, cook food)
  • Advanced hook launchers (Hookshots!)
  • Cosmetic armor (Hide your off-looking armor and show your skin to the world, or replace your armor with other models cosmetically)
  • Joy of Painting (Paint pixel art in minecraft)
  • Antique Atlas (Some fancy map)
  • Bountiful Baubles (ADMIN-ONLY baubles that grant powerful passives)
  • Animania (Cute animals to tame)

Building

  • Chisel (Adds a bazillion blocks)
  • Architecture Craft + Platforms (Adds alternative ways to shape blocks)
  • Crayfish Furniture + Decocraft + DarkRP Medieval (These add decorations)
  • Varied Commodities (ADMIN ONLY add decorations)
  • Rustic (Medieval-looking furniture and utilities)
  • Bibliocraft (Mostly wooden decorations and useful blocks like shelves or desks which hold items)
  • Signposts (Signs that can be rotated so they point somewhere)
  • Minetraps (For the most assholish)
  • Advanced lifting (Elevators!)
  • Ender storage (Adds color-coded chests which share items between them no matter the distance)
  • Iron chests (Adds chests that have more storage capacity)
  • Online picture frame (Print images and gifs from the net into Minecraft)
