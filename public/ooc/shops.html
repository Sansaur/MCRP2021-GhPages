Currently available RP shops

To acquire RP you can do the following, without being spammed (Remember that this isn't controlled by any kind of script as of now, so forgive human error):

  • Posting public roleplays with other players (+5 as base, bonuses can be applied)
  • Activity (+5 per day)
  • Commendations by other players (+2 per player, once)
  • Posting/Being part of faction-related or lore-related roleplays (+5 as base, bonuses can be applied)
  • Organising player events [Parties, competitions, field trips, etc...] (+10 as base, bonuses can be applied)
  • Posting / Being part of Faction-Faction interactions (+20 per RP / +5 if you're faction leader)

Acquiring NPCs: Before you can buy an NPC to live in your base, you must build them a suitable living space

Acquiring Special Buildings: These improve the NPCs, your PVP abilities, or your progression

Class/Faction powers: All of these require admin interventions, so understand if admins aren't available

FACTION-WIDE PURCHASES can be paid with a "bank" of points that are contributed by players or awarded by roleplay!!
