Solo Roleplay

Characters who aren't part of any major faction

Since they are people not tied to any kind of faction, they have certain limitations, here's the list:

  • Solo characters cannot acquire NPCs
  • Solo characters cannot acquire Faction buildings
  • Solo characters cannot use Faction powers
  • Solo characters can only have ONE deity at a time
  • Some Solo character class powers are nerfed:
  • Ancestral Healing costs 45 RP
  • Punishment costs 45 RP
  • Spirit Walk costs 90 RP
  • Evocation costs 30 RP
  • Informants costs 45 RP
  • Counterfeit costs 50 RP
  • Bribery is not available
  • Create scrolls only grants 2 scrolls
  • Portal is not available
