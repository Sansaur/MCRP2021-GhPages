Description

An vivid dark green Lizardfolk, a long lime fin trails down his spine from head to tail. worn tribal clothing. His armband bares the mark of exile among lizardfolk.

Backstory

Due to tragedies before Morsk's birth, his tribe had become distrustful of use of magic. In their younger years he had grown close friends with outsiders whom introduced and practiced magic although difficult and slow and extremely infrequent Morsk would become a novice in a decade. Which would be discovered and would strain their relations with the tribe until eventually exiled when the tribe hardened on their beliefs. Morsk would become an exile wanderer, raiding abandoned mages towers for new knowledge along the way.
