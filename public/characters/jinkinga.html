One of the most eye-catching attributes of his body is his face in general, having four eyes and a strange jaw that can split into two parts, poking out through his blue hair, there is a pair of small caprine horns, when he shows he teeth one could see how sharp and dangerous they look, specially the fangs. He has the ability to hiss with a pretty long, slender and nifty snake tongue. His legs are raptor-like, having scales instead of fur.
