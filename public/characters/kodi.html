Kodi

Race: Charr

Faction: Wizards (WIP)

Religion: Undecided

PVP Standing: Restricted

Description

A male anthropomorphic monstrous feline, standing 12 foot-tall, a mixture between a cat and a gnoll. His body shows a snow leopard's fur pattern, with the same depth to his fur. His frame is burly, covered in muscles that make him reach a weight of 600lbs (272kgs).
The sides of his head sport two pairs of bovine ears, one at each side, decorated with bright-colored piercings, from the cheeks two horns come out towards the sides and then they point forwards, while from the top of his head, two horns poke out pointing backwards.
The charr can walk either on two legs or as a quadruped, usually preferring this second method of movement over long distances.
