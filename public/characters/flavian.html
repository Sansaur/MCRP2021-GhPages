Flavian Tully (Seth Konbaung)

Race: Avian

Faction: None

Religion: Holy Revival of Eternal Workers of the Three Divines

Special role: None

PVP Standing: Wild

Description

A average height anthromorphic California Scrub-Jay. With patches of filth on him from machinery and mud.

Backstory

Flavian was a journeyman architect, working for a local entrepreneurial burghers using the latest technological advances to increase revenues of manufactured goods. Flavian grew up without any relatives and was raised by the community of the town, got in trouble alot in early life due to loss of direction of his life, spent his time mixing different beverages and over time his skill improved making money in the background to desperate alcoholics , Flavian a quiet fella who couldnt properly pronounce words properly, he than moved on in making small profits from beverages and went to work as assistant to a local airship explorer who taught him about machinery, to present time he escape with airship barely, as the forces of darkness and the coalition created his landlocked town a no man lands leaving only remnants.
