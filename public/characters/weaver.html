Description

A fuzzy spider with a very posh sense of fashion. Comes in biped and octoped flavors!

Backstory

A spider from another land, he claims to be a baron and have some great castle and his own town even! But he's never in any hurry to offer proof. He's come here looking to get back to working with his hands and enjoy a more modest life as a craftsman.

  • Free access to Grapple and Cobwebs spells
  • Cannot ever be targeted by PVP actions

  • Cannot ever use equipment from the Arbalester, Heavy Armors, Heavy Weapons or Thrower classes, no matter the circumstance
  • Cannot eat anything that isn't meat
