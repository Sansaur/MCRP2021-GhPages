Grit

Race: Kobold

Faction: Shrine Keepers

Religion: None

PVP Standing: Restricted

Description

Grit is a small red kobold without noticeable details, just over a meter, thin whit 24kg and always with an angry face. He is someone quiet but friendly, it is difficult for him to start conversations but he always answers them. Quite independent but works well in a team.

Backstory

Grit has not always been a lonely kobold, he belonged to a large family of the same race but they were terribly slaughtered by the disgusting humans. Now he prefers to live in a lonely desert that borders whit a beautiful and lush forest habited by animals and magical creatures that seem to take care of the place, that makes him feel secretly comfortable and he has decided to move not far from them.
