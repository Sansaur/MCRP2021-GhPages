Description

A female yellow Cat with white paws/hands also the tip of her tail is white, She has big purple eyes with 4 brown dots under each one, her nose is pink just like her "beans" and her hair is orange, standing 5,7" (1.75mts) with a weight of 165 lbs 75kg. She is a little hyperactive and competitive, some times she would like to go on adventure on her own and for some reason she is fascinated by dragons one of the reasons she is with the wizards.

Backstory

WIP - Short story: While exploring the world, Gatzumara, found some traces of a living dragon, meaning that she can study and interact with a dragon, so she decided to track it and "capture" it but she found that the dragon was part of a group of wizards. Instead of fighting them she decided to be part of this group to learn more about this world and dragons.
