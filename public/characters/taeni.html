Taeni Olatus

Race: Weedy Seadragon

Faction: Aristocrats (WIP)

Religion: Undecided

PVP Standing: Wild

Description

A male anthropomorphic seadragon standing a hair under 6 feet tall. His body is covered in green speckled fish scales with brilliant blue bands of iridescent scales lining his throat and chest. Numerous kelp like protrusions come off his almost painfully lanky body which judging by his graceful strut he seems to be awfully proud of.
