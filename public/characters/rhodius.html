Rho Rhodius

Race: Dog

Faction: Beastslayers

Religion: None

PVP Standing: Restricted

Description

He is a 1'65 m tall grey furred anthropomorphic dog with a fairly long hair, despite of his height he seems to be pretty strong

Backstory

Son of a blacksmith in his homeland fief.
Since he was a child he always showed skills in his father's workshop aswell as wielding heavy weapons and wearing heavy armor.
But it wasn't until he reached adulthood when he decided to arm himself and try luck traveling and working as a mercenary on his own.
A few years passed before he decided to abandon his nomad life to join the beastslayers in order to fullfill his gloryseeking ambitions.
