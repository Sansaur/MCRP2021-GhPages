Class / Faction powers

Marking buildings (FACTION)

Simply holds basic NPCs

Buying NPCs (FACTION)

Check "Acquiring NPCs"

Starting Faction V Faction (FACTION)

Check "PVP Rules"

Sending mercenaries (FACTION) (30 RP)

Allows to send an admin disguised as a random anonymous player to PK or to be an ally against a certain target, cannot participate in faction-scoped pvp

Psychopomp (SHAMAN) (15 RP)

Speak with the dead to gain information about their death circumstances and allow for ressurrection roleplays

Ancestral Healing (SHAMAN) (15 RP)

Fully restore a player's health, hunger and effects, anywhere

Punishment (SHAMAN) (15 RP)

Make lightning fall on a player, anywhere, if it can

Spirit walk (SHAMAN) (25 RP)

Teleport self somewhere and then return in a short lapse of time, you cannot interact with player-owned chests while doing this. Can't spirit walk to dungeons or places not visited before

Evocation (SHAMAN) (10 RP)

Receive ONE scroll that has any spell that can be casted by any spellslinger class, only the shaman himself can use this scroll, it's forbidden to be shared

Divination (SHAMAN) (15 RP)

Receive information if your faction, yourself, or someone specific, is the target of a private event, or will be the target of a private event throughout the day.

Bless (SHAMAN) (25 RP)

Enchants a target's held item with a low-level enchantment, to pick (if appliable) between Sharpness I, Efficiency I, Unbreakable I or Protection I

Informants (ROGUE) (15 RP)

Know the location of a player, this can be detected by other rogues near the target, so be wary

Counterfeit (ROGUE) (15 RP)

You will receive special currency that can be spent on black market shops

Bribery (ROGUE) (25 RP)

Temporarily let a member of your faction become friendly with a faction that should be hostile

Secret passages (ROGUE) (50 RP)

Teleport directly inside a target faction's base in a location that is safe and outside of view, if the faction has no buildings or safe spots this doesn't work. Can't target singular players until their homes are large enough.

Create scrolls (MAGIC.U) (50 RP)

Receive 4 copies of a spell you know in scroll form, it can be shared to other players

Learn new spells (MAGIC.U) (40 RP / 100 RP)

Learn a Novice or Apprentice spell with a single roleplay post or initiate a special quest to learn an Advanced spell within a Magic Location

Portal (MAGIC.U) (50 RP)

Teleport up to 6 players to a faction's base or the entrance of a dungeon. Must have a small structure in the shape of a portal be built.

New wand (MAGIC.U) (250 RP)

Attune to a new Apprentice wand and gain +2 max spell learned slots, you can have a maximum amount of 3 wands.
