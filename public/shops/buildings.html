Buildings that can be marked for a purpose

House (FREE)

Simply holds basic NPCs

Shop (FREE)

Needed for shopkeepers

Tavern (FREE)

Sometimes admins might put quest givers here

Cells (FREE)

Either this or a Prison are needed for kidnappings/keeping prisoners

Farm (FREE)

An admin may gift a minimum of 2 basic animals for a farm

Caravan (FREE)

Allows for fast travel between locations and counts as a stand for traders

Barracks (100 RP)

Increases siege defense tickets and improves the guard NPCs

Castle (300 RP)

Increases siege defense tickets and allows for Elite NPCs

Magic location (100 RP)

Needed for spell progression further than Apprentice

Temple (75 RP)

Improves deity favor as long as needs are met, needed for deity manifestations

Commerce Chamber (200 RP)

All traders get cheaper, they get more items on sale, and this generates ingame money

Workshop (150 RP)

Can call for artillery attacks during Sieges

Adventurer guild (150 RP)

Allows to hire bodyguards, and offers repeatable quests with good rewards

Dungeon / Prison (150 RP)

Allows to have both player and NPC prisoners, generates money off NPC prisoners as long as they stay there

Black Market (100 RP)

Allows to hire mercenaries, always has the special Rogue vendor within
