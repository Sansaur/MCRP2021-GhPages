NPC list

Farmers (15 RP)

They are item givers, they will offer basic kinds of food, depending on their living context (Porkchops / Wheat as examples) every 15 minutes

Miners (15 RP)

They are item givers, they will offer basic ores every 15 minutes

Healers (30 RP)

They will clear all status effects affecting a player, fully heal them, and then buff them with minor speed and absorption

Guards (20 RP)

Attacks hostile mobs and players from enemy factions, is EITHER melee or ranged, gets upgraded by military buildings

Traders (25 RP)

They will buy and sell items for ingame money, traders come in various types and they are never the same, traders may change stock and prices

Bankers (25 RP)

Exchange ingame currencies

Lords (150 RP) [ELITE]

All Farmers and Miners yield more (Only once), is a defensive yet quick melee combatant as well

Guardsmen (100 RP) [ELITE]

Very resilient and highly damaging slow melee/ranged combatant

Bishop (150 RP) [ELITE]

Full restore, just like an Healer. Combat healer that attempts to heal NPCs and players belonging to his faction from great range. Increases Favor for religion

Wizards (150 RP) [ELITE]

They sell scrolls for ingame money. Combat spellcaster
