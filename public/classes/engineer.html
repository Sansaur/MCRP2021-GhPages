Short description

A witty master of automation and tools, bringing innovations to lands once ruled by the fear of deities and wizards. Can use all of the matchlock guns and is able to teach the simplest ones to be used by any

Gains access to:

  • All of the Immersive Engineering contents
  • Crafting guns
  • Crafting airships
  • Crafting elevators
  • Crafting grappling hooks
  • Crafting Firebombs
  • Crafting Poison bombs
  • Crafting Smoke bombs
  • Stun grenades
  • Dynamite sticks
  • Explosive arrows
  • Using Cannons
  • Using Guns that require Medium or Strong ammo

Restricts vanilla to them:

  • Dynamite
  • Minecart dynamite
