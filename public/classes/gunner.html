Short description

The cherry on top of a great day is a feast

Gains access to:

  • Revolvers
  • Chem throwers
  • Railguns
  • Handguns
  • Shotguns
  • Miniguns
  • Rifles

Special passives:

  • Miniguns can ONLY be used while either immobile or in the backseat of a ViesCraft ship
  • Gunners can start with an handgun
  • Gunners may request half a stack of handgun ammo at any point as long as they are in their own faction's territory and they have no ammo left
