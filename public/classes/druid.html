Short description

Those greatly attuned to nature live among it, but few can reach enough trust from mother nature to be allowed to know it's secrets. Druids can protect themselves with heavy armor and cast both spells and rituals

Gains access to:

  • Sylvan armor
  • Wildwood armor
  • Druidic rituals
  • Druidry recipes (Roots mod)
  • Living tools
  • Wildwood bow and quiver

Restricts vanilla to them:

  • Bonemeal
