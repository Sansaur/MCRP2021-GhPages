Short description

Wielders of hammer, ember and hot iron, and materials better than iron of course. Their access to the Seared Forge allows them to duplicate ore yield

Gains access to:

  • Tinker's smeltery
  • Tinker tool crafting + replacing + modifying
  • Crafting heavy weapons
  • Crafting heavy armors

Restricts vanilla to them:

  • Anvil + Item repairs
