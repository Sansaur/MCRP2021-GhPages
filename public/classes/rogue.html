Short description

Sneaky assassins, silver-tongued manipulators and deceitful spies all fall under the label of the Rogue

Special passives:

  • They can change their ingame name and their model to another player's and enter hostile territories by impersonating them (Ask an admin for NPC immunity)
  • They can get special currency to spend in Black Markets and use an assortment of counterfeit items that aren't available normally
  • They have access to special RP powers (Check RP shops)
  • They can detect enemy informants attempts
  • They can steal ingame currency through pickpocketing (Check PVP rules)
