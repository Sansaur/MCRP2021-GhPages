Magic user progression

Magic users should NEVER get to progress and learn new spells by themselves, if you find a way this is done, report it

All Magic Users can learn both Novice and Apprentice spells, and they start the game with 2 spells known per magic school picked

To learn a new spell, the magic users must spend RP and post a "study" roleplay of why they are acquiring a new spell

Magic users can know up to 10 spells per magic school, even if their wands only allow for 5 to be carried

To change spells on their wands, Magic users need to contact admins and do it outside of combat or precarious situations

A Magic Location building + Completing a special roleplay is required for the following:

  • Improving wands from Basic to Apprentice, and from Apprentice to Advanced
  • Learning Advanced spells
  • Having multiple wands

Master spells can only be acquired after certain challenges have been passed
