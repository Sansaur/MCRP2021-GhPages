Other guidelines

Death

  • When you die you lose one Soul Ward, these protect your soul from true death
  • You can check your current amount of Soul Wards with the Deathspeaker NPC within the Ghost Town of the Pearlwood Cult
  • If they reach 0, you become a ghost, as a ghost you cannot deal damage, take damage or break blocks
  • While as a ghost you're only allowed to talk to players, you're forbidden from actually pursuing progress through PVE or farming
  • To resurrect:
    • A Shaman player must spend RP to acquire a Soul Ward item, these can be used to remove you from your Ghost status
    • You must be brought to a place that has a specific stone that can be used to draw on the powers of the undead to come back to life a limited amount of times
