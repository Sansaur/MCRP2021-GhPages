Religion creation

There can be a maximum of 1 religion per every 5 players on the server, a religion needs a minimum activity otherwise it's buried

A religion can have between 1 to 3 deities

If deities are pleased, they will give gifts, if they are pissed, they will cause godly rage

The following are the requirements to create a deity

  • Reign (Which power does the gold hold over)
  • Symbol (An image that must appear at temples)
  • Preferences (Which things does it like, which things does it hate)
  • Mannerisms (Defining ways the god acts)
  • Commandements (Rules that followers must follow)
  • Aliases (Deities can be copied by other religions in the form of aliases)
