General rules

Roleplay rules

  • 1 - Don't be a jerk
  • 2 - Don't grief
  • 3 - Obey the admins
  • 4 - Stay in character while on the server
  • 5 - Characters must have a readable-typeable name (Remember 16 characters max)
  • 6 - Report exploits, or even worse, exploiters
  • 7 - As long as you know of the presence of another player around you, slow down so they may roleplay with you
  • 7.5 - While not being watched you can be speedier and not have to roleplay everything you do
  • 8 - Follow the limitations of your jobs
  • 9 - Always roleplay and play like your life matters and is the only one you have
  • 10 - Do not use emotes (@.@ ._. XDXD) while roleplaying in character

Behaviour rules

  • 1 - Entrances to mines must have a very visible entrance
  • 2 - Do not make hostile mob farms
  • 3 - Do not make impenetrable bases
  • 4 - Do not destroy PVE dungeons

Special roleplay rules

  • A special roleplay is a character who will follow a more limited, yet specialised roleplay
  • They get access to permanent tools/buffs, earlier access to tools, or access to things they shouldn't have
  • They do not follow the class system, but must follow special rulings as given per special roleplay
