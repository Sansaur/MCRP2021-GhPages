PVP rules

Always-active rules

  • 1 - Any PVP situation can be screenshot and brought for admin supervision to see if rules were broken
  • 2 - All PVP situations require escalation beforehand
  • 2.1 - Escalation refers to the act of giving the player a heads up of the intent to PVP
  • 2.2 - Even further, a "signal" in the form of a roleplay post indicating the start of the attack is preferable before starting to swing ingame
  • 3 - Do not Godmode (Referring to the act of performing impossible feats ot describing events that happen in your favor without taking into account other players)
  • 4 - Do not Powerplay (Referring to the act of forcing another player to commit roleplay actions with your own posts)
  • 5 - Do not Metagame (Referring to the act of using information from outside the game to alter your actions)
  • 6 - Do not Revenge Kill (Referring to the act of pursuing whoever killed you immediatly after respawning)
  • 6.1 - If the killer belongs to a faction hostile to you, you cannot enter that faction's territories either
  • 6.2 - The span of time considered "immediately after getting killed" is 10 minutes
  • 7 - Do not destroy anything built by other players unless they (And everyone involved in it's construction) consent in it's destruction
  • 8 - Do not use the following tactics when fighting neither players nor NPCs:
  • 8.1 - Do not block paths with blocks, or, even worse, bits
  • 8.2 - Do not fight using buckets of liquid
  • 8.3 - Do not break mobs' pathing
  • 8.4 - Do not steal items from players' chests
  • 8.5 - Do not destroy other players' property
  • 8.6 - Do not gimp other players

How to deal with Premeditated aggression?

Restricted

You can pick either being fully immune to premeditated aggressions and never being a target of them. Or you can provide a whitelist of which actions you allow aggressors to perform onto you

Wild

You accept any consequences if you fall victim to premeditated aggressions and the aggressors may roleplay anything they wish even if later you still keep your rights to make it a canon event onto your character or not
